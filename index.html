Biography

Zihao Li received his B.S. and M.S. degrees from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, advised by Prof. Ting Chen (opens new window). He is currently working towards the PhD degree with the Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, under the supervision of Prof. Daniel Xiapu Luo (opens new window). His current research interests include Blockchain, Smart Contracts. He has received several best paper awards (e.g., INFOCOM'18, ISPEC'17, etc.) and a best paper nominee from ESEM'19.
